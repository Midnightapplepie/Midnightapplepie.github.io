Updating Site

Novemeber 16, 2014

Spent the entire weekend redoing my site, reviewing the files found lots of stuff that can be removed. After removing a whole bunch of unless codes, I started adding some back in, then later start redesigning the site and got myself into an endless hole.

It's a great review I have to say, as I was thinking about what to do with the background, I browse around for idea, I watch 9Elements.com's awsome video on youtube again and seeing how they can do amazing thing with html5 canvas, I somehow just started a project try to do a scaled down version of it. And it turn out to be an awsome project to review everything I've learned about Javascript so far.

The project was to draw circles on canvas, great review for making objects, and give them random size and random motions. I have used constructor, prototype, created random functions, timed motions, and recreate methods like shuffle and rand from Ruby to make things easier for my project. After creating the canvase, I then wanted to incorporate that onto my site and that how I really started redesign mysite to fit the canvas. The outcome was a lot of work and frustration and great amount of satisfaction in the end. It was nothing amazing but in the end, I am happy with what I got and have gotten better understanding on JS than I did 2 days ago. It was great.
